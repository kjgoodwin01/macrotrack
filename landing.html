How it works

Simple by design

1
Tell us your goal
Enter your stats and goal — cut, bulk, or maintain. We calculate your exact calorie and macro targets using your body weight, height, age, activity level, and goal rate.
2
Log your food daily
Search 600K+ foods, scan barcodes, take photos, or enter manually. Takes under 30 seconds per meal once you're in the habit.
3
Your coach analyzes the week
Every Sunday your AI coach reviews your entire week — weight trend, calorie adherence, protein consistency — and tells you exactly what to adjust going into next week.
4
Get your meal prep plan
Generate a personalized meal plan with exact weights for every ingredient. Print the shopping list, batch cook on Sunday, and your whole week is handled.
